About EBSB

"Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat" is an initiative taken by the MHRD whose motive was to pair different states and Union Territories of India and to ensure that they carry out a structured engagement with one another in the sphere of language, literature, cuisine, festivals, cultural events, tourism, etc.
According to the guidelines of MHRD, NIT Rourkela is paired up with VNIT Nagpur for the same.

— EBSB, NIT Rourkela

Our Mission and Vision

The main objective of our club is to celebrate the Unity in Diversity of our Nation and maintain and strengthen the fabric of traditionally existing emotional bonds between the people of our Country.

Our mission is to promote the spirit of national integration through a deep and structured engagement between all Indian States and Union Territories. To showcase the rich heritage and culture, customs, and traditions of either State to enable people to understand and appreciate India's diversity, thus fostering a sense of shared identity.
